WHISPERS

One Tuesday morning

…

a quiet server room was buzzing like a tame robot hive when an odd message
popped in: someone believed their microwave had learned to speak. After checking for errors and finding
none, the narrator answered with cautious curiosity, picturing a kitchen appliance whispering secrets.

The truth turned out hilariously normal. A Bluetooth speaker sat on top of the microwave,
rattling out voice notes and making the vents murmur. Once the speaker was removed, the “haunting” stopped, and the day
returned to its usual hum.

What Happened

:

A calm morning in a server room gets interrupted by a strange message about a “talking” microwave.

The sender hears whispers and thinks their kitchen appliance has developed a voice.

A photo reveals the real culprit: a Bluetooth speaker sitting on top of the microwave, buzzing whenever
messages come in.

Once the speaker is removed, the “haunting” ends, leaving the day's greatest mystery solved.
